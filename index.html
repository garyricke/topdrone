The Concept

What Is a
One Shot?

A "one shot" is an uncut, continuous FPV drone flight that tells the complete story of a location from entry to exit. No edits. No spliced clips. Just a single, perfectly executed run — and it takes weeks of training to pull off.

No Cuts

The entire video is a single continuous flight. What you see is exactly what was flown — there's nowhere to hide a mistake.

High Speed

FPV drones fly at up to 97 kph. Tight corridors, low ceilings, doorways at speed — every move requires thousands of repetitions first.

Intentional

Every turn, bank, and dive is choreographed in advance. The story is told through the flight path, not through editing after the fact.
